# Contact Us

Need help or want to collaborate? Pick the channel that fits:

- **Report a bug or issue** – submit an issue on our [GitHub repo](https://github.com/GetAdriAI/code-agent-docs/issues). Include your environment, steps to reproduce, and screenshots/logs where possible for faster turnaround.
- **Request a feature** – create a [feature request](https://github.com/GetAdriAI/code-agent-docs/issues/new?title=[Feature]%20Issue&labels=enhancement) with context on the business problem, expected workflow, and priority.
- **Talk about enterprise adoption** – drop us a note at [founders@getadri.ai](mailto:founders@getadri.ai) if you want to discuss implementations, roadmap partnership, or co-marketing ideas.
